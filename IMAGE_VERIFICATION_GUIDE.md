# Image Verification Methods - Now Available!

## Two Approaches for Maximum Security

Your Sentinel.ai now has **two ways to verify suspicious images**:

---

## **1. REAL-TIME WEBCAM SCAN** (During Call)
**When to use:** When you see a suspicious image on the caller's screen during the call

**How it works:**
1. Click **"📸 SCAN SUSPICIOUS IMAGE"** button
2. Camera opens - show the phone screen/document to your webcam
3. Click **"⚠️ DETECT THREAT"** to analyze
4. Instant result: FAKE or SAFE

**Pros:**
- ✅ Real-time during call (no waiting)
- ✅ Immediate alert & email notification
- ✅ Automatic threat detection

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ Must position image at right angle to camera
- ⚠️ Quality depends on camera clarity

---

## **2. MANUAL IMAGE UPLOAD** (After Call)
**When to use:** Upload an image after the call to verify it later, or test suspicious documents

**How it works:**
1. Click **"📁 UPLOAD IMAGE TO VERIFY"** button
2. Select image file from your device
3. System analyzes and shows detailed report
4. See confidence score, indicators, and analysis

**Pros:**
- ✅ Crystal clear image analysis (no camera blur)
- ✅ Detailed breakdown of what makes it fake/real
- ✅ Can re-analyze as many times as needed
- ✅ Save and review results

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ Requires extra step after call
- ⚠️ Not real-time (but thorough)

---

## **Effectiveness Comparison**

| Factor | Webcam Scan | Manual Upload |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| **Speed** | ✅ Real-time | ⚠️ Post-call |
| **Accuracy** | ✅ Good | ✅✅ Excellent |
| **Image Quality** | ⚠️ Depends on camera | ✅ Perfect clarity |
| **Detail Level** | ✅ Good | ✅✅ Very detailed |
| **Best For** | During call | Verification after |

---

## **Best Practice: Use BOTH**

1. **During call:**
   - Use **REAL-TIME WEBCAM SCAN** for instant detection
   - Get immediate alert & email notification
   - Set off alarm if threat detected

2. **After call:**
   - Use **MANUAL UPLOAD** for detailed verification
   - Check what made the image suspicious
   - Save results for report

---

## **What Gets Detected (Both Methods)**

### ✅ FAKE/SUSPICIOUS:
- AI-generated IDs (obvious artifacts)
- Photoshopped documents (visible edits)
- Poor quality fakes (pixelated, blurry)
- Wrong government seals/logos
- Multiple typos/misspellings
- Payment QR codes in suspicious context

### ✅ REAL/LEGITIMATE:
- Actual government IDs (even if poor photo quality)
- Legitimate documents
- Real app screenshots
- Images with minor quality issues but correct details
